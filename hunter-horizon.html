Hunter Horizon

An action RPG made with Unity inspired by Monster Hunter and Sea of Thieves.
I made the game sounds, using Musescore for the music and Audacity for the sound design. I made a custom audio system with various features such as adaptive music.
I also worked on the graphics, making a custom shader for a fully procedural sky.

The project was made in about 8 weeks during 2023 and 2024.
